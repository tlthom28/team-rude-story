Hallo mein nam ist Rammstein. The hund named Bratwurst went to the flughafen. Bratwurst saw a baeckerei in the flughafen and bought himself a aapfelkinchen and Doener. Suddenly, a man in Lederhose passed by and offered him a piece of Brot. Ich besuche Schloss Eltz am Wochenende. Ich esse eine Bratwurst im Biergarten. The man in lederhosen saw a wettbewerbsfaehig woman with her hair blowing in the Klimaanlage, fell in love, and the rest was history.
