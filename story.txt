Hallo mein nam ist Rammstein. The hund named Bratwurst went to the flughafen. Bratwurst saw a Baeckerei in the Flughafen and bought himself an Apfelkuchen and a Doener, then walked down the Hauptstrasse with his snacks. Suddenly, a man in Lederhose passed by and offered him a piece of Brot. Ich besuche Schloss Eltz am Wochenende. Ich esse eine Bratwurst im Biergarten.
